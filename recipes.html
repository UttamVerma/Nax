PANNER TIKKA MASALA

Ingridients

2 Tbsp Oil

Chopped onion

Chopped tomato

Chopped garlic

Chopped Ginger

Garam Masala Powder

Coriander Powder

Coriander Powder

Preperation

heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan

add 1 chopped onion

1 chopped tomato

4 chopped garlic cloves and 1½ teaspoon chopped ginger

sauté until it turns golden brown.

add 1 cup of water and bring it to boil

add the cooked tikkas

sprinkle ½ teaspoon of garam masala and coriander powder each

boil the contents for 4 min

garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or paratha
